excerpt: I had low expectations for a Upper East Side sushi spot, but it was better than I expected. The sushi rice was good and warm, and the fish quality was solid. My main gripe was they abused truffle. Like, they put some on uni which is a bewildering choice. I know they were probably going for fancy, but the truffle killed uni's delicate flavors. Still, I'd go back if I'm in the area since it was reasonably priced. Oh, and get the nigiri—their rolls were meh.
