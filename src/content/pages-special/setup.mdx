## Apps

### Firefox

I stopped using Chrome after the reduced adblockers abilities.

### Irvue

It changes your desktop wallpaper on your chosen cadence with [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/) images. It keeps your desktop feeling fresh.

### Rectangle

Mac window management with keyboard shortcuts *chef kiss*

### 1Password

My password manager of choice. You gotta be using one in this day and age.

### Basic Mac apps for Notes, Reminders

I’ve tried more feature rich apps for both, and I find I always go back to the simplicity of these apps. Apple does a good job locking you in since they’re on every Apple device.

### Lightroom

I don’t really like Adobe or its products, but I haven’t made the switch to something else quite yet.

### markEdit

For writing markdown. I’m writing this post with it right now!
