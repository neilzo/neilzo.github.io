The Curated Record

Favorites

A strictly edited list of works that have fundamentally shifted my perspective on aesthetic and mechanical utility.

01.

Cinema

Movies & TV
2001

Stalker

Andrei Tarkovsky

A metaphysical journey through a forbidden landscape. Its architectural pacing and deliberate stillness redefine the boundaries of visual narrative.

1982

Blade Runner

Ridley Scott

The definitive study in neon-noir world-building. A masterclass in atmosphere, where the environment serves as a primary emotional protagonist.

2019

Succession

HBO

A brutal exploration of power and familial collapse, choreographed with the precision of a classical tragedy through hyper-modern dialogue.

02.

Games

Interactive Logic
2019

Disco Elysium

ZA/UM

A revolutionary approach to narrative choice and internal character dialogue. An oil-painted fever dream of political philosophy and human frailty.

2011

Dark Souls

FromSoftware

Interconnected level design that functions with the logic of a gothic cathedral. A landmark in atmospheric storytelling through environment.

2016

The Witness

Thekla, Inc.

Pure geometric epiphany. A dialogue between the player and the island's landscape, stripped of all traditional UI distractions.
