Section 01 / Bio

I explore the intersection of structural precision and human narrative. My work is a continuous attempt to document the ephemeral through a disciplined architectural lens.

Based at the edge of the digital and physical, I curating moments of quiet intensity. This space serves as a living monograph of my interests—ranging from brutalist photography to the evolution of design systems. I believe that clarity is not the absence of complexity, but the successful management of it.
